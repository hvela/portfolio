A bit about myself,

    I am a current undergraduate at George Mason University majoring in Information Technology with a concentration in Database and Technology Programming. I am also an IT Intern at Fairfax County Governement. Before my time at Mason, I attended Northern Virginia Community College, where I obtained my AS in Business Administration. I held a position as an Assistant Store Manager and Analyst working with Procurement and Logistics for governemnt contracts. My passion for IT grew during both positions as I was constantly challenged to train and learn various software systems. This ultimately helped me take the right path and pursue a BS in Information Technology. On my spare time I enjoy traveling, sharing/learning IT knowledge, staying active, and playing guitar.
